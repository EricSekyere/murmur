    Welcome to Murmur

    Private, on-device dictation. Speak and your words appear in whatever app you're using — nothing leaves your machine.

    • Double-tap Right Ctrl to start and stop
    • Or press your hotkey ctrl+shift+space
    • Say "new line", "new paragraph", or "scratch that" while dictating

    Test your microphone

    Click the mic, say a sentence, then pause. Your words should appear below.
